Our Story

A Course Born
from the Wild

Rimoi Golf Course sits on the floor of the Kerio Valley, where the Elgeyo Escarpment rises 1,800 metres into the sky. Every round is played against one of East Africa's most breathtaking natural amphitheatres — ancient volcanic ridges, riverine forest, and the distant shimmer of the Kerio River snaking through the valley below.

Founded in 2022 by conservationists and golf enthusiasts, Rimoi was designed to honour — not override — the landscape. The 18-hole course weaves through indigenous bush, with fairways framing views of the Rimoi National Reserve, where elephant herds, Grevy's zebra, and hundreds of bird species move freely across the valley floor.

We are 45 kilometres from Eldoret and a stone's throw from Iten — Kenya's legendary "Home of Champions", where Kalenjin distance runners have shaped global athletics. The spirit of discipline, endurance, and wild beauty that defines this region lives in every corner of the course.

The Course

Two distinct nines — each shaped by a different face of the Kerio Valley — unite for one of Kenya's most memorable rounds of golf.

01

The Escarpment Nine

Holes 1–9 climb the lower reaches of the Elgeyo Escarpment, rewarding bold tee shots with panoramic views across the entire Kerio Valley. Narrow fairways thread through indigenous acacia and fever tree groves. Every approach demands precision — and nerve.

Holes 1–9 · 3,680 yards
02

The Kerio Nine

Holes 10–18 descend to the valley floor, tracing the contours of the Kerio River basin through open savanna and riparian forest. Wildlife corridors bisect several fairways — elephant, giraffe, and bushbuck are not unusual playing companions on the back nine.

Holes 10–18 · 3,820 yards
03

Practice Facilities

A floodlit 300-yard driving range, two practice putting greens, and a dedicated short game zone. The Rimoi Golf Academy offers PGA-affiliated coaching for all skill levels — beginner to elite amateur — with private and group lessons available daily from 6 AM.

Beyond the Course

Experiences

🐘
Wildlife Safaris

Guided game drives into the Rimoi National Reserve. Spot elephant herds, Grevy's zebra, leopard, and over 400 bird species — all within minutes of the 18th green.

🌅
Sundowner Golf

Play the back nine as the Kerio Valley turns gold. Evening tee times timed to sunset over the escarpment — an experience found nowhere else in Kenya.

🏢
Corporate Events

From team-building scrambles to full tournament days for 144+ players. Our events team handles catering, branding, prizes, and transfers from Eldoret or Nairobi.

⛳
Junior Academy

Weekend programmes for ages 8–17, with PGA-qualified coaches, free equipment rental, and a pathway to national junior rankings. Nurturing Kenya's next golf generation.

🍽️
Fine Dining

The Kerio Terrace serves contemporary East African cuisine. Farm-to-table menus featuring local Elgeyo produce, fresh Kerio River tilapia, and signature Kenyan single-origin coffee.

💆
Spa & Wellness

The Rimoi Wellness Sanctuary draws on traditional Kalenjin healing botanicals. Post-round massages, steam rooms, and a plunge pool with unbroken escarpment views.
